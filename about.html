About

Two voices. One mission. Reigning over darkness with the Word.

Joshua

"Reigning over darkness — Isaiah 45. Instrument in the hands of Him who reigns."

Preaching, church, and the Word. Mic and Bible in hand — bringing Scripture to life through sermon and song. Shepherd of the flock and co-founder of Cyrus Reigns Records.

Simion

"Enlightenment through sound. Guitar and the light of the Word."

Guitar and sound. In the dimly lit room with light streaming in — symbolizing the light of the Word. Co-founder of Cyrus Reigns Records, bringing the rock edge to Scripture-driven music.

Biblical inspiration

Cyrus Reigns Records exists to proclaim the Word through music and spoken word. Our first EP draws from Ezekiel, Isaiah, and Jeremiah — shepherds, kingdom, and the burden of the Lord.

We believe in one King, one righteousness, and one day when every knee will bow.

Why Scripture?

We set Scripture to music and spoken word because the Bible is living and active — it shapes faith and culture. Each track starts with the sermon and the text, then the Shakespearean rhymed version adds another way to hear and remember the message.

The Word does not return empty.

We want you to listen, then open the Bible and read the passage for yourself.
