Book Us in Your Town

Church, venue, or event — here’s everything you need to book Cyrus Reigns Records.

Booking rate

$15,000 per booking. One flat fee covers the performance. Travel, lodging, and technical requirements are outlined below.

How to book

  1. Email us at hello@cyrusreignsrecords.com with the subject line “Booking request” and your venue name, city, and preferred date(s).
  2. We’ll reply within a few business days with availability and a simple agreement.
  3. Sign and deposit. A deposit (typically 50%) secures the date; the balance is due before or on the day of the show.
  4. Confirm details. We’ll agree on set length, sound check time, and any special requests (merch table, meet & greet, etc.).
  5. Show day. We arrive, set up, play, and leave your audience encouraged by the Word and the music.

What’s included

  • Full band performance (set length agreed in advance).
  • Professional sound within our rider (we can send a technical rider after your initial inquiry).
  • Merchandise available for sale at the event (optional).
  • Travel and lodging are the responsibility of the host (we’ll send a standard rider with requirements).

Technical & hosting

We’ll provide a technical rider (stage plot, input list, backline needs) after we confirm your date. Typical needs include a clear stage area, basic backline or DI options, and a competent sound engineer. For travel and lodging, we ask for hotel accommodations (or equivalent) for the band and crew for the night of the show (and travel nights when applicable).

Exact details are in the rider we send upon booking confirmation.
